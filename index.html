NewParadigm Capital Markets

A leading independent corporate finance advisory firm focused on providing financial advice on mergers & acquisitions, restructuring exercises, capital raisings and strategic advisory.




Our Story

Established in 2005, NewParadigm Capital Markets is an award winning, independent and privately-owned corporate finance advisory firm licensed by the Securities Commission of Malaysia.

Who We Are

NewParadigm Capital Markets is in the niche business of providing independent, unconflicted financial and strategic advice on capital raising, financings, recapitalisation & restructurings and mergers & acquisitions to a diverse client base.

Our Founding Partners

Our Founding Partners have an aggregate of more than 40 years of experience assisting corporate clients in solving complex, strategic and financial issues. The active involvement of our senior partners ensures that their expertise and experience is capitalised on when advising our clients and when executing day-to-day assignments.
